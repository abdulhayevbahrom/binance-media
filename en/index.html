      Frequently Asked Questions.
      What is a cryptocurrency exchange

      Cryptocurrency exchanges are digital marketplaces that allow users to buy and sell cryptocurrencies, such as Bitcoin, Ethereum, and Tether. Binance is the largest cryptocurrency exchange by trading volume.

      What products are listed on Binance?

      Binance is the world's leading cryptocurrency exchange, serving 150 million registered users in over 180 countries. With low fees and the ability to trade 350+ cryptocurrencies, Binance is the preferred exchange for trading Bitcoin, altcoins, and other virtual assets. With Binance, users can:

    • Trade hundreds of cryptocurrencies on spot, margin и futures contracts.
    • Buy and sell cryptocurrency with Binance P2P.
    • Earn interest on your cryptocurrencies with Binance Earn.
    • Buy or earn new tokens on Binance Launchpad.
    • Trade, invest, stake and borrow NFTs on the marketplace Binance NFT.

      • How to Buy Bitcoin and Other Cryptocurrencies on Binance

      There are several ways to Buy Crypto on Binance. To Buy Crypto on Binance, you can use a credit/debit card, fiat balance, or Apple Pay/Google Pay. Before you get started, make sure you have completed the process of verifying your personal information on your Binance account.

      How to Track Cryptocurrency Prices

      The simplest way to keep track of the latest cryptocurrency prices, trading volumes, popular altcoins, and market capitalization is through the Binance Cryptocurrency Catalog. Click on a coin to view its historical price, 24-hour trading volume, and real-time cryptocurrency price, such as data on Bitcoin, Ethereum, BNB, and others.

      How to earn with cryptocurrency on Binance

      Users can earn rewards on more than 180 cryptocurrencies using the products offered on Binance Earn. Our platform features dozens of digital assets such as Bitcoin, Ethereum, as well as stablecoins.

      How to trade cryptocurrency on Binance

      You can trade hundreds of cryptocurrencies on Binance using spot trading, margin trading, futures contracts, and options contracts. To start trading, users need to register an account, complete identity verification, purchase/deposit cryptocurrency into their account, and execute a trade.
